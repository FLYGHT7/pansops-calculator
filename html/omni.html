PANS-OPS Formula Reference

PDG Formula

PDG = (((obs − (der + 5)) ÷ dist) + 0.008) × 100 [%]

OIS Elevation

OIS = (der + 5) + (dist × 0.025) [m]

OIS = (DER + 5 m) + (dist × 2.5%)

MOC Clearance

MOC = dist × 0.008 [m]

MOC = 0.8% of horizontal distance

Required Altitude

ReqAlt = obs + MOC [m]
